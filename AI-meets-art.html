AI Meets Art: Friend or Foe
FROM THE AUTHOR:
AI Meets ART: Friend or FOE explores the impact of Artificial Intelligence on Art, demystify common misconceptions surrounding AI technology, discuss the ethics and legality of its use as a powerful tool and debate whether AI image sampling is akin to stealing or taking inspiration.
We address the fear art communities feels towards AI, and list actual examples of how traditional artists are earning an income today by capitalizing on the imperfections of AI-generated Art.
Lastly, AI Meets ART: Friend or FOE emphasizes how AI can be a threat to many livelihoods but may also enhance human talent and create new industries, providing a roadmap for independent studios who want to stay at the forefront of technological innovation in a rapidly-changing world.
